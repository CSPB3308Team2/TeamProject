How Todo Loco Works:

Todo Loco allows users to plan out their day with the objective of completing all tasks as quickly as possible without.

When entering a task on the "Todos" page, fill in the task you would like to add to your schedule, then click the map icon inside the task box. Find your location on the map and click it!

Just like that, your task and a visualization of the footraffic for that location will be added!
